What To Do This Week
1Pre-treat
Mix a small amount of No. 1 Hydrate RX with distilled water and smooth it through dry hair.
2Hydrate
Use No. 1 Hydrate RX and let it sit for 10 to 15 minutes.
3Cleanse
Cleanse with No. 2 Fortify RX, starting at the scalp and working through the hair.
4Treat
Use No. 1 Hydrate RX for 7 to 10 minutes in Phase 1 or No. 3 Bond RX for 5 to 7 minutes in Phase 2 and 3, then rinse well.
5Support
Use No. 4 Vita Boost RX on damp hair.
6Style
Style in a way that feels comfortable and stay consistent each week.

Wash once a week. Move through it gently.

Consistency creates the result.

Daily Care & Styling

The way you wear your hair between washes determines how well it holds over time.

Between Wash Days
•Cover your hair at night with silk or satin
•Avoid excessive heat or restyling
•Do not add heavy oils or outside products
•Use a small amount of No. 6 Silken RX if needed for smoothness
•Keep manipulation low — less is more
Styles That Align With Your Phase
1Hydration
Loose buns, low ponytails, wraps, soft styles
2Internal Strength
Silk press, soft curls, clip-ins, controlled styling
3External Strength
Sleek ponytails, bobs, protective styles with low tension
Protect the work between washes.
